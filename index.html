About Hydro-IOT

The Smarter Way to Track Water Resources

Hydro-IOT is an intelligent, scalable, and responsive ecosystem designed to tackle global water management challenges. Combining ultrasonic water level detection, automated water flow control, and web-based telemetry, we bring unparalleled transparency to residential, commercial, and industrial facilities.

We install robust, weather-resistant ultrasonic sensors that measure distance to water levels accurately without physical contact, minimizing corrosion and maintenance.

Sensor logs are encrypted and transferred to central databases. Historical reports are generated instantly to discover and model usage habits over weeks or months.

Hydro-IOT prevents overflows and shut-offs through automatic scheduling algorithms and instantaneous alerts sent to your phone and building manager portals.
